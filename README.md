# Bright-AutoMobile-Car-Sales
#🚗 Bright Motors Car Sales Analysis
#📌 Overview

##This project analyzes vehicle sales data for Bright Motors to uncover insights that drive revenue growth, optimize inventory, and improve regional performance.

##🎯 Objectives
. Identify top-performing car makes and models
. Analyze pricing, mileage, and manufacturing year relationships
. Evaluate regional sales performance
. Understand customer preferences (fuel type trends)
. Provide actionable business recommendations

##🛠️ Tech Stack
. Databricks (SQL)
. Excel / Power BI (Visualization)
. GitHub (Version Control)

###🔄 Data Processing
. Converted price fields to numeric
. Removed inconsistencies and null values
. Created calculated metrics:
.Total Revenue
. Profit Margin
. Margin Categories

##📊 Key Insights
###🚘 Revenue Drivers
. A small group of car models contributes the majority of revenue
. 🌍 Regional Performance
. Certain regions dominate sales, indicating expansion opportunities
. ⛽ Customer Preferences
. Fuel type trends show shifting demand patterns

###💰 Profitability
. High-margin vehicles are not always top-selling but drive profitability

###📈 Dashboard Features
. Revenue by Make & Model
.Sales by Region
. Revenue Trends Over Time
. Fuel Type Distribution
. Price vs Mileage Analysis
. Interactive filters (Year, Region, Fuel Type)

###💡 Recommendations
. Increase stock for high-demand models
. Expand operations in high-performing regions
. Promote high-margin vehicles
. Align pricing strategies with mileage and year trends
##🚀 Outcome

.This analysis provides data-driven insights to support strategic decisions for increasing revenue and operational efficiency at Bright Motors.
